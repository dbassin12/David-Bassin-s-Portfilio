Bio

I spent a decade teaching History and Economics, where I learned that the best systems don't replace human judgment — they support it. Now I help people learn what AI can actually do for them, and I build the systems to prove it. My current setup — Baleph, an AI chief of staff I configured on the OpenClaw platform — runs five agents and 16+ automated pipelines 24/7 at near-zero cost, from job search agents that filter 85% of irrelevant roles to a voice-to-CRM pipeline deployed for a real client. That teaching background is the whole point: a 17-point scoring error caught by human override, not AI, proved that guardrails aren't optional — they're the product.

How I Work

Three principles, applied in sequence.
